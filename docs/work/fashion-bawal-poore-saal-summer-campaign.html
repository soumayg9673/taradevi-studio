Fashion Bawal Poore Saal — Summer Campaign

Creative DirectionTrend ForecastingVisual StorytellingStrategic PositioningConcept DevelopmentAesthetic Curation

The "Fashion Bawal Poore Saal" campaign is built on the philosophy that "bawal" (disruption/impact) doesn't take a break just because the temperature rises; it simply gets hotter. For this summer edition, we are stripping away the "fancy" filters and leaning into a Raw, Editorial Street-Vibe that captures the relentless energy of kids and youngsters who own the streets regardless of the sun.

The narrative is simple: while the world slows down in the heat, our audience ramps up the chaos, turning sweaty, sun-scorched days into a high-fashion statement.

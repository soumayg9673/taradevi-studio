Winter Campaign: Global Aesthetic Scaling

Art DirectionCreative StrategyCampaign ProductionSocial Content Scaling

A "luxury-to-mass" winter campaign bridging global aesthetics with high-volume social storytelling..

Plan

  • Conceptualized visual mood boards and storyboards, defining the aesthetic direction, lighting references, and color stories for the entire winter season.
  • Collaborated with Art Directors and Photographers to translate brand concepts into high-impact visual assets, ensuring every shot met the campaign's creative brief.
  • Curated and mapped 200+ outfits, managing the technical "flat lay" and "on-model" styling to ensure consistency across the digital lookbook.
  • Led model scouting and talent selection, personally vetting and casting talent that embodied the brand's winter persona.
  • Directed on-site location scouting, identifying and securing environments that aligned with the storyboard's atmospheric requirements.
  • Orchestrated the production of 100+ creative assets, overseeing the transition from raw footage to social-optimized reels with a focus on high-retention hooks.
  • Managed the end-to-end creative workflow, from initial outfit planning and artist briefings to final post-production and asset delivery.

Strategy

  • Elevated the brand's perception in Tier 2, 3, and 4 markets by benchmarking art direction against global luxury standards, effectively bridging the gap between high-end international aesthetics and local accessibility.
  • Strategized the use of foreign talent and premium styling to offer a "window into luxury" for regional audiences, differentiating the brand from the utilitarian imagery typically used by competitors in these territories.
  • Produced 100+ creative assets to fuel an aggressive social calendar, ensuring the brand dominated the "share of voice" on platforms like Instagram and Facebook during the peak winter shopping window.
  • Designed a dual-track shooting workflow that enabled the simultaneous capture of cinematic brand reels and an editorial photoshoot, maximizing asset output without doubling production time.
  • Curated a specific "look and feel" that combined high-fashion foreign model casting with relatable winter styling, creating a unique "Global-Local" (Glocal) identity that felt both premium and reachable for regional consumers.
  • Cleverly utilized high-end art direction and foreign casting to "price up" the brand's perception, allowing a mid-market product to compete for the psychological mindshare of a luxury consumer.
  • Architected a high-gloss visual narrative that suggested an international budget while maintaining a lean, high-output production model—forcing competitors to overspend to match our visual standard.

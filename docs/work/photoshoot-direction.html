Personal Project

Editorial Photoshoot Direction

PhotographyArt DirectionStyling

Presentation

Directed and art directed editorial photoshoots that earned the Best Photograph award. Each shoot was carefully planned with mood boards, lighting concepts, and detailed styling briefs.

Plan

  • Created detailed mood boards drawing from fine art and editorial references
  • Developed lighting diagrams and location scouting notes for each concept
  • Briefed photographers, stylists, and MUAs with a unified visual direction
  • Selected and retouched final selects for print and digital submission

Strategy

  • Prioritised authentic, unretouched skin and natural light to contrast over-produced industry norms
  • Submitted to competitions with strong conceptual narratives to stand out from technical-only entries
  • Archived every shoot into a structured portfolio for future brand pitches

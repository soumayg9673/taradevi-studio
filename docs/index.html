Creative Strategist · Visual Strategy & Production Lead · Creative Producer · Brand Storytelling

What truly makes my heart feel alive is art

—

As a young creative with over five years of experience, I’ve naturally grown into a multidisciplinary storyteller working across art direction, visuals, films, and campaigns, mostly within retail and e-commerce. My journey began in Assam, where I was born and raised, eventually leading me to study Mass Communication and Video Production at St. Anthony’s, Shillong. Since childhood, I have been deeply drawn to photography, film, and creative fashion—realms that allow me to live in a different world. Along the way, I’ve worn many creative hats—from Creative Director to Designer and Advertising Specialist—working with brands like Myntra and Citykart. These experiences have shaped my intuitive, hands-on way of working. I’m drawn to creating work that not only makes me proud but also sparks that “wow” feeling in others.

Fashion Bawal Poore Saal — Summer Campaign

The "Fashion Bawal Poore Saal" campaign is built on the philosophy that "bawal" (impact) doesn't take a break just because the temperature rises; it simply gets hotter. For this summer edition, we are stripping away the "fancy" filters and leaning into a Raw, Editorial Street-Vibe that captures the relentless energy of kids and youngsters who own the streets regardless of the sun. The narrative is simple: while the world slows down in the heat, our audience ramps up the chaos, turning sweaty, sun-scorched days into a high-fashion statement.

05

Dilon Ka Tyohar — Festive Campaign

“Dilon Ka Tyohar” is more than a tagline; it is our brand’s heartbeat. It stems from a simple truth: while clothes are draped over the body, they are always chosen by the heart. In this campaign, we redefine the act of shopping. It is no longer a transaction; it is the "First Celebration"—the joyous, expectant prelude to the festivities. We position our marketing designs as the threshold where the festival begins, and our films as the mirror reflecting a family’s shared dreams.
